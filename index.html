Nested

NFTs that can own other NFTs

Build digital artworks with a design made up of multiple elements that can be activated, deactivated, transferred or sold in your own gallery of collectible items.

Let's imagine an NFT collection of Mr. Potato Head. Users could have a basic version of the potato body and purchase some unique add-ons like dragon ears, hats, or a different background.

Info

Hypermedial

NFTs that can own other NFTs with multiple resources

This feature allows a single NFT to contain multiple formats (.jpg, svg, gif, video, audio…) and be displayed in different ways, depending on the digital environment where it appears (platform, web, app, metaverse, peripheral devices...). You can customize your NFT to have many functionalities and can easily progress through any type of metaverse or transmedia narrative.

They are upgradeable NFTs that allow new resources to be added after they have been minted. This guarantees that any work can continue to evolve and adapt to new scenarios.

Info

CONDITIONALS

NFTs with dynamic layers that connect with external data

Digital items that are connected to any event that can be verified by querying external data through an oracle.

For example, Imagine a NFT collection of polar bears programmed to show an angry face every time the CO2 emission into the atmosphere increases or cry if the Bitcoin price drops. Any atmospheric, social, or political event can affect your NFT.

Transversal

NFTs that can be used in multiple collections

A digital item that can be added to different characters from different NFT collections. Imagine a limited edition of 10 hats that are transferable and wearable between different collections. Although these hats are a shared resource between collections, there can only be 10 NFTs equipped with this hat.

An artist or collection can collaborate with another work or collection by putting some of its elements. This allows users to create more open narratives without knowing what is coming next, or planning it in advance.

Info

Governance

NFTs governed by multiple users

A set of people has the power to decide on a NFT collection.

Imagine a digital artwork created collectively or governed by a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization). In this case its final appearance will depend on the votes of the community. The resulting image can be traded or sold as an NFT. The resulting royalties are shared with the community of owners.

Info

AI

NFTs with personality

An NFT can have its own intelligence that will be unique and personal. It can have its own digital brain, of greater or lesser complexity, that allows it to appear as a chatbot, or it can be guided by a random algorithm that reacts unpredictably with interactions or with any atmospheric, social or political stimulus.
